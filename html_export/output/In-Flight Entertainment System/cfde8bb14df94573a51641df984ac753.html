Play Airline-Imposed Videos

SystemFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > System Analysis > System Functions > Root System Function > Perform Cabin Management Activities > Play Airline-Imposed Videos

This function consists in triggering the play of pre-recorded airline videos. In particular, these imposed videos inclure pre-flight safety instructions (detailed explanation given before takeoff to airline passengers about the safety features of a commercial aircraft). Such imposed videos can also be commercial ads.
