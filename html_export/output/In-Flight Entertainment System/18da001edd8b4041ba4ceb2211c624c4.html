Audio announcements are information about the flight provided by the cabin crew or the captain (boarding, pre-flight announcements, captain's announcements, safety briefing, turbulence, take-off/ascent, descent/final, landing, etc.). The passenger can listen to these annoucements aither through the aircraft speakers either through the individual Seat TVs.
