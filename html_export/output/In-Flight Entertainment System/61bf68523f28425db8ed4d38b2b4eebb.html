Broadcast Audio Video Stream

LogicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Logical Architecture > Logical Functions > Root Logical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio and Video Diffusion > Broadcast Stored Audio and Video > Broadcast Audio Video Stream

This function consists in transmitting audio and video streams to one or several Seat TVs and or to the cabin screens. The implementation of this function is likely to be based on streaming solutions.
