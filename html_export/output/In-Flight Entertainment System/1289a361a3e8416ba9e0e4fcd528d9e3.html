Synchronize Audio Video

PhysicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Physical Architecture > Physical Functions > Root Physical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio Video Diffusion > Display Video and Play Audio > Synchronize Audio Video

This function is responsible for providing the decoding functions with the synchronization information needed to play back the audio and video streams together. In most cases, the file header, most of the metadata and the synchro chunks are specified by the container format.
