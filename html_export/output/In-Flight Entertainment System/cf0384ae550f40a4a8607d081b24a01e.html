Broadcast Stored Audio and Video Streams

PhysicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Physical Architecture > Physical Functions > Root Physical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio Video Diffusion > Broadcast Stored Audio and Video Streams

This function is responsible for broadcasting existing audio and video media. Transmitting audio and video streams means:

- Sending them to each passenger (on Seat TVs)

- Sending them to the cabin screens
