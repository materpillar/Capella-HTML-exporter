Split Audio Video Streams

PhysicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Physical Architecture > Physical Functions > Root Physical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio Video Diffusion > Display Video and Play Audio > Split Audio Video Streams

Encoded audio and video streams are typically assembled in a container bitstream such as MP4 or FLV. This function is in charge of splitting the two streams that are going to be decoded separately.
