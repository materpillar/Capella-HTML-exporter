Receive and Bufferize Audio Packets

PhysicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Physical Architecture > Physical Functions > Root Physical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio Video Diffusion > Display Video and Play Audio > Play Audio on Seat TV > Receive and Bufferize Audio Packets

As the data packets arrive, they are buffered for a few seconds and then decoded for the playback to begin. Bufferizing the audio packets reduces the risk of playback interruption due to network congestion.
