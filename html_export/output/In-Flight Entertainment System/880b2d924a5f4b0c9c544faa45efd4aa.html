Play Video Stream on Cabin Screen

PhysicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Physical Architecture > Physical Functions > Root Physical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio Video Diffusion > Display Video and Play Audio > Play Video Stream on Cabin Screen

This function receives video packets and restitutes the corresponding video through the cabin screens. The audio track associated to the video is forwarded to the aircraft through another function.
