Broadcast Stored Audio and Video

LogicalFunction

In-Flight Entertainment System > In-Flight Entertainment System > Logical Architecture > Logical Functions > Root Logical Function > IFE System > Manage Audio and Video Diffusion > Broadcast Stored Audio and Video

This function is responsible for broadcasting existing audio and video media. Transmitting audio and video streams means:

- Sending them to each passenger (on Seat TVs)

- Sending them to the cabin screens
